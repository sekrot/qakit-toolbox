## English (en)

**Short (123 chars):**

> 13 offline developer & QA utilities in Chrome's side panel: JSON, JWT, regex, hashes, base64, diff, screenshots and more.

**Detailed:**

> QAKit Toolbox is a single side-panel home for the small utilities every QA engineer and web developer reaches for ten times a day — without leaving your browser and without sending your data anywhere.
>
> **New in 0.2.0:** Password Generator and a JSON → YAML converter, plus a rebuilt Screenshot editor with blur, step numbers, emoji stamps, curved arrows, and full undo/redo.
>
> **Thirteen tools, zero network calls:**
>
> • **JSON Formatter** — format, minify, validate, and query with JSONPath (with autocomplete)
> • **JWT Decoder** — split, decode, and check expiry/not-before of JSON Web Tokens
> • **Base64 / URL** — encode and decode, with auto-direction detection
> • **Unix Timestamp** — convert between epoch and human-readable dates, any timezone
> • **UUID Generator** — v4, v7 and NIL, batch generation
> • **Regex Tester** — live match highlighting, capture groups, full cheatsheet
> • **Hash Calculator** — MD5, SHA-1, SHA-256, SHA-512, for text or files
> • **Text Diff** — side-by-side and unified, ignore whitespace/case
> • **Clipboard History** — auto-capture, search, pin important items
> • **Color Picker** — pick any color on screen, copy as HEX/RGB/HSL
> • **Screenshot** — capture the active tab, annotate with rectangles, highlights, arrows, lines, freehand pen, multi-line text, crop
> • **Password Generator** — strong random passwords with length, character-set, and exclusion rules
> • **JSON to YAML** — convert JSON to YAML, copy or download the result
>
> **Built for QA workflows:**
> • Available in 6 languages: English, German, French, Spanish, Russian, Italian
> • Light/dark/system themes
> • Persistent history that survives Chrome restarts
> • Keyboard shortcut to open the side panel anywhere
> • Export/import all your settings as one JSON file
>
> **Privacy first:**
> Everything runs locally. No analytics, no telemetry, no servers. Your data lives only in this extension's sandboxed storage. We can't read it, and neither can anyone else.
>
> Open source ethos — no upsells, no accounts, no tracking. Just the tools you need, one click away.
